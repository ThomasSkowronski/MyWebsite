This is a simple dice game

To play: roll the dice and then decide if you would like to roll again.
If you get too many strikes across all of your rolls (equal to the number of dice) then your score is 0 and that round is over.
Click 'bank points' in order to record your score and try again.
This page was written using JQuery and Bootstrap.
